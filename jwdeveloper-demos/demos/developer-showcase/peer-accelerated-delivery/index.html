Streamroot peer-to-peer video CDN

Streamroot is a webRTC-based P2P approach to video delivery for online broadcasters. Combining traditional CDN delivery and distributed delivery protocols, Streamroot offloads demand from servers by safely and securely connecting viewers watching streams simultaneously. Streamroot delivers 20 million video sessions every day with major media companies across the globe. Its solutions are encoding, CDN and DRM agnostic, plugin-free for viewers and integrate seamlessly into broadcastersâ€™ existing workflows.

Benefits of Streamroot delivery include:

  • Higher quality of service thanks to sourcing from a local mesh network
  • up to 75% lower CDN costs
  • Greater scale for large live events and globally dispersed audiences
  • Transparent & secure delivery across devices

Streamroot uses JW Player's modular framework to create a custom provider, and can be integrated into your JW Player with a single line of code.
